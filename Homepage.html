2023 -> 2024

With the rebirth of the society after Covid 19, we started moving into the main design and initial build of our new submarine, Tsunami. With initial testing and building of the hull and tail sections. As the year progressed, we moved forwards within the design process from electronics to propulsion. With the end of 2024, we started the assembly of our electronics, alongside final testing to hull and propulsion build methods

2022

With the end of Covid, we finally managed to use our submarine Tempest and show its off in its second race in all it's glory at the eISR submarine races.

2019 -> 2021

Whilst there wasn't much development during this period due to Covid, there were still some highlights despite the large break created from the studying from home environment, with the summer of 2019 seeing the first race for Tempes!, bringing a good visit for SUHPS to the states for the internation submarine races in florida.
